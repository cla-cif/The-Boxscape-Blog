What is Boxscape?

BY CLA-CIF | Founder

Hey there!

The Boxscape is a blog for people whose ideas break out of the box (escape), it's a place for independent thinkers.
How do you know if this blog is right for you?
An independent thinker is someone who analyses situations as they see fit, rather than conforming to public opinion. They evaluate multiple perspectives, gather evidence, ask questions and use their critical thinking skills. Even when their decisions contradict the opinions of others, independent thinkers remain convinced of their values.
At Boxscape, we believe in freedom of expression and in respecting the opinions, identities, preferences, religions and cultures of others. Everyone is welcome as long as they respect each other's uniqueness, dignity and freedom. We like people who contribute to making this a safe space.

Code of Conduct

Be kind and courteous
We're all in the same boat to create a welcoming environment. Let's treat everyone with respect. Healthy debate is natural, but kindness is needed.
No hate speech, bullying, abusive behaviour
Make sure everyone feels safe. Bullying of any kind isn't allowed and derogatory remarks about things like ethnicity, religion, culture, sexual orientation, gender or identity won't be tolerated.
No advertising or spam
Give more than you take in this group. Self-promotion, spam and irrelevant links aren't allowed.
Respect the privacy of others
Participation in this group requires mutual trust. Authentic, expressive discussions make groups great, but can also be sensitive and private. What's shared in the group should stay in the group.
Use only English.
To ensure that all group members understand you, please use English only.
Stay on topic.
We only allow posts and comments that are related to the activities of the group. Off-topic content will be removed and you'll receive a warning.
Report rule violations.
If you see a post/comment that breaks our rules, report it to the admins - DON'T verbally attack the author of the post/comment.
No promotion of terrorism
We adopt the UN definition of terrorism as any act "intended to cause death or serious bodily harm to civilians or non-combatants for the purpose of intimidating a population or coercing a government or international organisation to perform or refrain from performing an act".
No violation of the law
This means strictly NO Promotion/Sharing/Enactment of:
Self-harm, Drugs & Dangerous Products, Terrorism, Violence, Hate & Harassment, Sexual Content, Deception & Minisformation, Impersonation & Malicious Content, No Copyright Infringement/Content Ownership.

Enforcement

We will investigate reports of potential violations of our Code of Conduct and, if we are able to confirm such reports, we will take action against the relevant users. Our enforcement actions can include education, warnings, temporary suspensions, and in the case of more serious violations a permanent ban. We will share our enforcement decision with the person against who the violation was filed. To respect privacy, we will generally not disclose enforcements to the wider community, but reserve the right to do so when it is in the public interest.
